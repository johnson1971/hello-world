The Fallen

Unfortunately, there is no way to list all of our fallen brothers and sisters that we have lost to police brutality. The names are too numerous, even if only going back a few months or years. There is also anonymity with many of them. So many of their stories are never told, names never becoming a hashtag, pictures are never shown, identity unknown. For some, this list is just a list of names, but for others its a reminder of how America needs to do better, to ensure the list grows shorter.
